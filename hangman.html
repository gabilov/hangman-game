How to Play

01

CHOOSE A CATEGORY

Choose a category. First, choose a word category like animals or movies. The computer then randomly selects a secret word from that topic and shows you blanks for each letter of the word.

02

GUESS LETTERS

Guess letters. Take turns guessing letters. The computer fills in the relevant blank spaces if your guess is correct. If it's wrong, you lose some health, which empties after eight incorrect guesses.

03

WIN OR LOSE

Win or lose. You win by guessing all the letters in the word before your health runs out. If the health bar empties before you guess the word, you lose.
